How It Works

Three simple steps from instruction to completion.

1

Describe Your Task

Type a natural language instruction into the FSB chat panel. "Book a flight from SF to NYC" or "Find the cheapest laptop on Amazon."

2

FSB Analyzes & Acts

FSB reads the page, plans actions, and executes them. Clicking, typing, scrolling, and navigating as needed.

3

Task Complete

FSB verifies the result, reports back in the chat, and gives you a summary. Review the outcome and iterate if needed.
