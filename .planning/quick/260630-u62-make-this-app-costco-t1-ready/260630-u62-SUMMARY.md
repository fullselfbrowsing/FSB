# Summary

Made Costco T1-ready for the safe public ecommerce read subset.

Costco now has three executable T1a read handlers backed by the public no-auth `https://ecom-api.costco.com` ecommerce APIs through `executeBoundSpec`, with `authStrategy: none`, `credentials: omit`, and origin pinned to `https://www.costco.com`.

## Promoted Slugs

- `costco.get_product`
- `costco.get_products`
- `costco.get_product_availability`

## Excluded Slugs

Kept Costco account, list, cart, checkout, navigation, rendered-search, geocode, write, and destructive rows out of handler proof. In particular, `costco.add_to_list`, `costco.create_list`, `costco.delete_list`, `costco.geocode_location`, `costco.get_current_user`, `costco.get_list_items`, `costco.get_lists`, `costco.navigate_to_cart`, `costco.navigate_to_checkout`, `costco.navigate_to_product`, `costco.navigate_to_search`, `costco.remove_list_item`, and `costco.search_products` remain non-promoted.
