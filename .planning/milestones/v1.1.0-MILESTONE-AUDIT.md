# v1.1.0 -- T1 App Execution Expansion -- Milestone Audit

**Status: PASSED.** All 17 v1.1.0 requirements are checked complete, phases 44-51 are roadmap-complete and verified, and the final Phase 51 closeout keeps the catalog honest: descriptors are either proven T1/guarded or explicitly accounted for with the proof still required before direct execution. No critical blockers were found.

## Definition of Done

**Goal:** move FSB from catalog/search/discovery support toward verified direct `invoke_capability` execution where technically and safely provable, without weakening MV3 Wall 1, Wall 2, the denylist/sensitive-origin posture, or the two-tool MCP surface.

**Achieved:**

- Readiness inventory over all **2,314** descriptors with explicit current status and proof requirements.
- Shared T1 port contract and CI gates for handler proof, origin pinning, shape guards, no-secret logging, guarded writes, and app-tool-surface drift.
- New executable read coverage through Netlify, Bitbucket, CircleCI, Vercel, Retool, Asana, and generated low-risk same-origin reads.
- Pattern-D and GAPI candidates kept fail-closed unless/until bridge proof exists.
- Write/destructive activation moved behind live UAT evidence and a verifier-backed ledger.
- Final Phase 51 terminal-state report accounts for every descriptor without claiming the full catalog is T1-ready.
