<objective>
Teach the serve-owned supervisor to interpret owned-server topology with strict ownership, cold-first selection, and no-replay fallback.

Purpose: OpenCode attach must be an optimization over an FSB-owned verified process, never a discovery mechanism or a reason to replay a task.
Output: One generic daemon server lease, exact readiness/health ownership validation, fresh per-task children, and deterministic idle/shutdown/recovery behavior.

<scope_rationale>The lifecycle state, authentication/health proof, and teardown/recovery races share one authority owner and one deterministic fake-process/clock harness. Splitting them across unrelated modules would create an attachable lease before ownership or cleanup was proven.</scope_rationale>
</objective>
